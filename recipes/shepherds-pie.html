Sheperd's Pie

Description

Shepherd's pie is the ultimate all-in-one dish. It's got everything you could ask for in a meal: hearty meat, starchy potatoes, colorful veggies, and gooey cheese.

Ingredients

  • Ground Lamb
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Peas
  • Cheese
  • Flour
  • Beef Broth
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes until tender than mash them.
  2. Cook the vegetables.
  3. Cook the ground lamb.
  4. Add flour, ketchup, and broth to the lamb and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. In a casserole dish, add your lamb, vegetables, mashed potatoes in that order. Finally, add your cheese.
  6. Bake in the oven until golden brown.
